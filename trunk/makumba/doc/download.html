Download locations

Makumba source code and binaries are mirrored around the globe to assure availability even in most unexpected things (hurricanes, crashes, network blackouts...) should occur.

Primary download locations

  • http://www.makumba.org/download
  • http://sourceforge.net/project/showfiles.php?group_id=25704
  • http://download.makumba.org/makumba/download/

Backup download locations

  • http://si.makumba.org/download
  • http://ee.makumba.org/download
  • http://ro.makumba.org/download

Local distribution point

  • download/ (only available on makumba.org mirrors)
