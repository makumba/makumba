!Introduction

One of the most common operations in database-driven websites is to display information to the user. Makumba provides a query-based approach to this through its [mak:object|MakumbaObject] and [mak:list|MakumbaList] tags.

!Displaying data through queries

Both [mak:object|MakumbaObject] and [mak:list|MakumbaList] use the same general principles for displaying data. [mak:object|MakumbaObject] is meant to handle only one object, while [mak:list|MakumbaList] handles multiple objects. In the following introductionnary examples, we will use [mak:object|MakumbaObject] in order to explain the general mechanism.
